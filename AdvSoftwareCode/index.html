About Celiac

Celiac disease, caused by gluten in wheat, oats, and barley, and is one of the most popular allergens experienced by a large number of people, causing infected many symptoms and serious complications that significantly affect immune and digestive health which causes severe cell atrophy, stress, disruption, sleep problems, poor growth, facial palate, and anaemia.
Celiac Website will help you to book a nutritional consultation or to find alternatives for food you like.
