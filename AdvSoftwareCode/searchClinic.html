Nutritionist Linda Nutrition

Professional Background

Linda Nutrition Center is the first center in Jordan and the Gulf specialized in international programs to increase, decrease or stabilize weight .. The Linda Nutrition Center was distinguished from others. Nutrition was launched in Jordan in 2016.
The programs of the Linda Nutrition Center are characterized by being healthy programs from eating at home without additional costs away from hunger and deprivation during the period of the program. The center’s programs also focus on dropping weight gradually from fat mass without losing muscle mass because this is important in maintaining the healthy structure of the body without harming Or negatively affect the body and its immunity in addition to changing the nutritional habits of the center’s visitors to reach a healthy lifestyle through guidance and weekly follow-up.

Education
